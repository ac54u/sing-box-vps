### 🔐 技术细节

- 日志函数签名：`log(){ echo "[$(date +'%Y-%m-%d %H:%M:%S')] $*" | tee -a "$log_file"; }`
- 证书目录：`/etc/s-box/certs/`
- 日志目录：`/var/log/sing-box-vps/`
- 日志文件：`/var/log/sing-box-vps/install.log`
